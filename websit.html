About DigiSkills.pk

DigiSkills.pk is the largest Training Program in Pakistan offering best Free Online Courses in freelancing Skills with E-Certificates issued by VU and Ignite. Over 4.0 Million + trainings have been impartedsince 2018 to generate skilled workforce in Future of Work and to strengthen digital economy of Pakistan.
